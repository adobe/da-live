Photoshop on mobile includes a wide selection of free features to accomplish all the core photo editing capabilities. Whether you're new, curious, or already familiar with Photoshop, we’ve made it easier than ever to learn and expand your creative skills.

Photoshop on mobile simplifies your creative & design needs:
⦁\tAdd new objects
⦁\tBlur or remove backgrounds
⦁\tReplace backgrounds and remove unwanted objects
⦁\tRetouch, refine and perfect your images with targeted adjustments
⦁\tCombine multiple images to create high-quality compositions and explore intuitive AI tools
⦁\tCreate unique collages, album cover art, perfect your passion projects, and develop unique digital art—all in one place

There is no limit to what you can create.

KEY FEATURES
⦁\tREMOVE OR REPLACE BACKGROUNDS
⦁\tEffortlessly select the background with the Tap Select tool.
⦁\tEasily replace backgrounds with an image directly from your phone, create AI-generated backgrounds with Generative Fill, or select from a large library of Adobe Stock images, including textures, filters and patterns.
⦁\tAdjust the background, including the brightness, effects or vibrancy to bring your creations to life.

REMOVE UNWANTED DISTRACTIONS
⦁\tBrush out blemishes, spots, or small imperfections in seconds using the Spot Healing Brush.
⦁\tQuickly and easily remove unwanted content from your images with our powerful Generative Fill feature.

PERSONALIZED IMAGE DESIGN
⦁\tCreate stunning visual images that are uniquely yours by blending photos, graphics, text, utilizing effects and more.
⦁\tCombine unique elements from your own photos with a selection of free Adobe Stock images, including textures, filters, fonts and patterns, to elevate your final creations.
⦁\tEffortlessly select an object or person with the Tap Select tool.
⦁\tRearrange objects in your image and control how they come together with layers.
⦁\tEasily add and remove content from your photos using simple text prompts with Generative Fill. Additionally, ideate, create new assets, and jumpstart your creativity using Generate Image.

BRING COLOUR AND LIGHT TO LIFE
⦁\tAdjust the colour of anything, like your shirt, pants or shoes, using adjustment layers. Use the Tap Select and other selection tools to perfectly edit the brightness or vibrancy to add a pop of colour to your images.
